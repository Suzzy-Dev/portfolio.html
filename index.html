01 // About

Dark Star,
Liverpool

I'm Suzy — 27, Liverpool-based, and entirely uninterested in being ordinary. I live at the intersection of obsessive detail, dark aesthetics, and a deep love for things that most people don't bother to understand properly.

I spent time working at PlayStation — hands down the best job I've had — and walked away with a level of insight into the games industry that doesn't come from the outside. What it also gave me was a very clear picture of what it feels like when something you love becomes a workplace, for better and for worse.

These days I pour that same obsessive energy into my own projects — hardware builds, self-hosted tech, and a media setup I'm genuinely proud of. When I'm not deep in a project or a playlist, I'm probably having a conversation that goes three hours longer than anyone expected, or walking Buddy while pretending I enjoy being outside.

I think slowly, thoroughly, and in first principles. I don't do surface-level — not in music, not in aesthetics, not in anything I actually care about.
